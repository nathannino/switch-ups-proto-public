[center][font_size=27]Actions requesting additional information[/font_size][/center]

Some actions will ask additional information, such as which spirit the effects of the action will apply to.

[img]res://manual/images/en/actinfo.png[/img]

A description of what to select will be at the top of the screen, and the available options will be at in the middle of the screen.

If the action requests information in the middle of a turn, you will be forced to select an option. Otherwise, you can press the back button to cancel the action.

[font_size=25]Note[/font_size] :
When putting your cursor on the upper left button, you will get a description of what part of the action this selection will affect.
